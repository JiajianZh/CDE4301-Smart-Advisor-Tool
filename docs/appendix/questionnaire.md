## RIASEC Questions (24 questions, 4 per type)

All questions use a 5-point Likert scale:
- 1 = Strongly Disagree
- 2 = Disagree
- 3 = Neutral
- 4 = Agree
- 5 = Strongly Agree

---

### Realistic (R) Questions

**R1:** I enjoy working with tools, machines, or equipment.

**R2:** I like building or fixing things with my hands.

**R3:** I prefer practical, hands-on activities over theoretical discussions.

**R4:** I would enjoy working outdoors or in a workshop environment.

---

### Investigative (I) Questions

**I1:** I enjoy analyzing problems using logic and data.

**I2:** I like conducting research to discover new knowledge.

**I3:** I prefer solving complex puzzles or abstract problems.

**I4:** I would enjoy working in a laboratory or research setting.

---

### Artistic (A) Questions

**A1:** I enjoy expressing myself through creative work (art, writing, design, music).

**A2:** I like thinking of new and original ideas.

**A3:** I prefer unstructured, flexible environments over strict routines.

**A4:** I would enjoy working on creative projects that allow artistic freedom.

---

### Social (S) Questions

**S1:** I enjoy helping people solve their problems.

**S2:** I like teaching or explaining concepts to others.

**S3:** I prefer working with people rather than working alone.

**S4:** I would enjoy a job that involves understanding human behavior.

---

### Enterprising (E) Questions

**E1:** I enjoy leading teams or managing projects.

**E2:** I like persuading or influencing others.

**E3:** I prefer competitive environments where I can achieve goals.

**E4:** I would enjoy a job that involves business strategy or entrepreneurship.

---

### Conventional (C) Questions

**C1:** I enjoy organizing information or maintaining detailed records.

**C2:** I like following clear procedures and established systems.

**C3:** I prefer structured tasks with well-defined expectations.

**C4:** I would enjoy working with data, numbers, or databases.

---

## Values Questions (5 questions)

Students rate how important each value is to them using the same 5-point scale. Only ratings of 4 or 5 are used for matching.

**V1:** **Financial Security** - Earning high income and having stable financial prospects is important to me.

**V2:** **Social Impact** - Helping people and making a positive difference in society is important to me.

**V3:** **Creative Freedom** - Having opportunities for innovation and creative expression is important to me.

**V4:** **Technology** - Working with cutting-edge technology and scientific advancement is important to me.

**V5:** **Work-Life Balance** - Having time for personal life and maintaining healthy work-life balance is important to me.

---

## Question Development Notes

**Source:** Questions adapted from the O*NET Interest Profiler (U.S. Department of Labor, public domain).

**Adaptations made:**
- Simplified technical vocabulary for pre-university students
- Removed culturally specific references (e.g., "farm equipment" → "technical systems")
- Adjusted for Singapore educational context
- Shortened questions for mobile-friendly display

**Pilot testing:** Five friends completed the questionnaire and provided feedback on clarity and wording. Revisions were made based on their suggestions.
